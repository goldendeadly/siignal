# Targeting Plan for SolarEdge Pro — Premium Rooftop Solar Installation

## Campaign Overview
- **Campaign Duration:** June 1 – August 31, 2026
- **Total Budget:** $15,000/month ($45,000 total)
- **Platforms:** Facebook/Instagram, Google Ads, YouTube, NextDoor
- **Marketing Objective:** Lead Generation (book free energy audits)
- **Resonance Mode:** Authority (data-driven, trustworthy)

---

## 1. Facebook / Instagram

### Audience Segments
- **Primary Homeowners in Sun Belt states**  
  - **Criteria:**  
    - Age: 35–60  
    - Income: $85,000+  
    - Location: Texas, Arizona, Florida, California, Nevada  
    - Homeownership: Owns home (exclude renters)  
    - Interests: Home improvement, renewable energy, savings, tech gadgets  
    - Behaviors: Engaged with eco-friendly or home upgrade content, recent homebuyers or home improvement shoppers  
  - **Rationale:** Matches target persona’s demographics, psychographics, and geographic focus.

- **Retargeting Segment**  
  - **Criteria:** Website visitors within the last 90 days, especially those who viewed solar or energy audit pages.  
  - **Rationale:** Warm audience for higher conversion probability.

- **Lookalike Audience**  
  - **Criteria:** Based on existing leads or customer database, 1-2% lookalike in target states.  
  - **Rationale:** Expand reach to similar high-quality prospects.

### Geographic Targeting
- **Primary Areas:** TX, AZ, FL, CA, NV
- **Exclusions:** Areas with limited sunlight or where installation restrictions apply (if applicable).

### Budget Allocation
- **Primary Ads:** $10,000/month (~67%)  
- **Retargeting:** $3,000/month (~20%)  
- **Lookalikes:** $2,000/month (~13%)  

### Schedule
- **Start/End:** June 1 – August 31, 2026
- **Dayparting:** 8 AM – 8 PM local time (peak engagement hours)
- **Notes:** Boost posts and run A/B tests on different creative assets.

### Optimization Goals
- **Primary:** Lead form submissions (book free audit)  
- **Bidding Strategy:** Cost per lead (CPL) optimization, manual CPC with bid caps if available.

---

## 2. Google Ads

### Audience Segments
- **High-Intent Search Keywords:**  
  - "solar installation near me"  
  - "solar panel cost 2026"  
  - "residential solar quotes"  
  - "solar energy savings"  
  - "best solar panels"  
  - "solar financing options"  
  
- **Display Retargeting:**  
  - Visitors to website in last 30 days  
  - Engaged with solar content or calculators

### Geographic Targeting
- **Areas:** TX, AZ, FL, CA, NV (same as above)  
- **Exclusions:** Limited sunlight regions, if relevant.

### Budget Allocation
- **Search Campaigns:** $9,000/month (~60%)  
- **Display Retargeting:** $3,000/month (~20%)  
- **Keywords & Bidding:**  
  - Max CPC: $3–$5  
  - Focus on high-intent keywords with quality score ≥7

### Schedule
- Continuous, optimized for business hours (8 AM – 8 PM)  
- Adjust based on performance data.

### Optimization Goals
- **Primary:** Conversions (form fills for free audit)  
- **Strategy:** Manual CPC, target CPA <$45, adjust bids based on device/location.

---

## 3. YouTube

### Audience Segments
- **Target:**  
  - Homeowners in Sun Belt states, 35–60, interested in home improvement, energy savings, renewable energy.  
  - Based on in-market audiences for home improvement and energy solutions.
- **Retargeting:**  
  - Users who watched previous videos or visited site in last 30 days.

### Schedule
- June 1 – August 31, 2026, with higher bid levels during evenings (6–9 PM).  
- Frequency capping to avoid ad fatigue.

### Budget Allocation
- **Video Ads:** $1,500/month (~10%)  
- **Content focus:** Trust-building testimonials, speed, warranty.

### Optimization Goals
- **Primary:** Lead clicks to booking page  
- **Strategy:** Cost per view (CPV), optimize for conversions.

---

## 4. NextDoor

### Audience Segments
- **Local Neighborhoods:**  
  - Residents aged 35–60, household income $85K+, interested in home upgrades.  
  - Focus on communities with active homeowner associations.
- **Retargeting:**  
  - Users who engaged with previous neighborhood ads or visited site.

### Geographic Targeting
- **Areas:** Specific neighborhoods within target states, radius-based targeting (~10 miles).  
- **Exclusions:** Commercial or rental properties.

### Budget Allocation
- **Community Ads:** $1,000/month (~7%)  

### Schedule
- June 1 – August 31, 2026, with emphasis on weekends and evenings.

### Optimization Goals
- **Primary:** Engagement and lead forms  
- **Strategy:** Cost per lead, local trust signals.
