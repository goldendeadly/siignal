# SolarEdge Pro — Optimization & Expansion Plan  
*Campaign Duration: June 1 – August 31, 2026*

---

## 1. A/B Testing Opportunities

### a. Headlines & Hooks
- **Hypothesis:** Shorter, question-based headlines will increase CTR.
- **Variants to Test:**
  - "Tired of Rising Bills? Save Now." (Control)
  - "Want to Cut Your Electricity Costs? Find Out How." (Test)
  - "Save $2,400/year — Ready to Go Solar?" (Test)
- **Metric:** Click-through rate (CTR), Cost per Lead (CPL).
- **Expected Outcome:** Question hooks and concise messaging will boost engagement by 10–15%.

### b. Call-to-Action (CTA) Variations
- **Hypothesis:** "Book Free Audit" vs. "Get Your Free Solar Quote" will influence conversion.
- **Variants:**
  - "Learn More"
  - "Book Your Free Audit"
  - "Get Your Free Quote"
- **Metrics:** Lead form submissions, CPL.
- **Expected Impact:** Clear, action-oriented CTAs will improve lead quality and volume.

### c. Creative Formats
- **Hypothesis:** Video testimonials outperform static images.
- **Variants:**
  - Real homeowner testimonial video
  - Static carousel of installation photos
- **Metrics:** Engagement rate, conversions.
- **Expected Impact:** Video will increase trust and engagement by 15–20%.

### d. Audience Segments
- **Hypothesis:** Retargeting warm audiences yields higher conversion than cold audiences.
- **Variants:**
  - Website visitors (last 30 days)
  - Engaged social media users
- **Metrics:** Conversion rate, CPL.
- **Expected Impact:** Retargeted audiences will convert 1.5–2x more efficiently.

---

## 2. Retargeting Strategies

### a. Website Visitors
- **Segment:** Users who visited solar info or booking pages in last 90 days.
- **Offer:** Emphasize quick installation and warranty to address objections.
- **Ad Types:** Dynamic display ads, testimonial videos, reminder messages.
- **Budget:** 20% of total ad spend (~$3,000/month).

### b. Engaged Social Users
- **Segment:** Users who interacted with ads, comments, or shares.
- **Offer:** Highlight promotion (free smart monitor), trust signals.
- **Ad Types:** Carousel ads, testimonial videos.
- **Budget:** 10% of total ad spend (~$1,500/month).

### c. Lookalike Audiences
- **Segment:** Based on existing leads and customer data.
- **Approach:** 1-2% lookalike in target states.
- **Offer:** Focus on immediate savings, fast install.
- **Budget:** 10% of total (~$1,500/month).

### d. Special Retargeting Campaigns
- **Abandoned forms:** Remind users of limited-time promotion.
- **Roof concerns:** Address safety and warranty assurances.

---

## 3. Cross‑Sell & Up‑Sell Opportunities

- **Additional Offerings:**
  - Solar monitoring and maintenance packages.
  - Battery storage solutions (for energy independence).
  - Upgrading to higher-efficiency panels or additional arrays for larger homes.
- **Targeting:** 
  - Past clients and leads who completed initial consultation.
  - Users expressing interest in energy independence or home upgrades.
- **Messaging:** Emphasize long-term savings, energy resilience and increasing home value.

---

## 4. Automation & Tool Enhancements

### a. Automated Bidding & Budget Allocation
- **Tool:** Use platform AI (Google Smart Bidding, Facebook Automated Rules).
- **Function:** Adjust bids based on time-of-day performance and audience engagement.
- **Benefit:** Maximize CPL efficiency and reduce manual oversight.

### b. Creative Rotation & Testing Scripts
- **Tool:** Use platform A/B testing automation or third-party scripts.
- **Function:** Rotate ad variants automatically based on performance thresholds.
- **Benefit:** Maintain fresh creative and optimize engagement without manual intervention.

### c. Lead Management & Reporting
- **Tool:** Integrate CRM and analytics via Zapier or platform APIs.
- **Function:** Automate lead follow-up, scoring, and reporting.
- **Benefit:** Streamline lead nurturing and performance tracking.

### d. Dynamic Retargeting
- **Implementation:** Use pixel data to serve personalized ads based on user behavior.
- **Benefit:** Increase conversion likelihood by tailoring messages to user intent.

---

## 5. Next Steps & Implementation

- **Immediate:** Launch A/B tests for headlines, creatives, and CTAs as outlined.
- **Within 2 weeks:** Set up retargeting campaigns for warm audiences.
- **Within 1 month:** Deploy automation tools and scripts.
- **Ongoing:** Monitor performance bi-weekly, iterate on top-performing variants, and adjust budgets accordingly.
