### Reflections Document (`performance-reflections-v1.md`)
```markdown
# Performance Reflections — SolarEdge Pro Campaign (June 2026)

## Summary of Metrics
- **Overall Impressions:** 35,500
- **Total Clicks:** 1,540
- **Average CTR:** 4.34%
- **Total Conversions (Bookings):** 610
- **Average CPC:** $0.89
- **Average CPA:** $5.87
- **Estimated ROAS:** 4.2x (based on average lead value of $200)

## Insights & Lessons Learned
- **Ad Performance:**  
  The Facebook carousel and Google Search ads delivered the highest engagement, driven by clear value propositions and high-intent keywords.  
- **Messaging Effectiveness:**  
  Trust signals like real installation photos and warranty emphasis resonated well, boosting CTR and conversions.  
- **Creative Feedback:**  
  Video testimonials on Instagram Stories showed promise but could be optimized with more targeted messaging about speed and warranty.  
- **Targeting & Audience:**  
  Demographic targeting aligned well; retargeting segments showed a 15% higher conversion rate, indicating warm audiences respond better.  
- **Challenges:**  
  CPC on Google remains competitive; exploring more long-tail keywords could reduce costs.  
- **Next Steps:**  
  Increase budget allocation to top-performing ads, test different testimonial formats, and refine retargeting audiences for higher ROI.

## Recommendations
- Continue emphasizing warranty and speed in messaging.
- Focus on retargeting warm leads for higher conversion.
- Test new creative assets with stronger calls-to-action.
- Monitor CPC trends closely and adjust bids accordingly.

*Note:* All messaging complies with regulatory guidelines, including "results may vary" disclaimers.
```
