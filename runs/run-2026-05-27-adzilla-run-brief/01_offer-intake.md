- High-efficiency Tier-1 panels maximize energy production and savings

- Average system cost: $28,000 before incentives

- **Performance Warranty:** Reduces risk and enhances trust, emphasizing durability and long-term savings

- **Desired Outcomes:**
  - Lower monthly energy costs immediately
