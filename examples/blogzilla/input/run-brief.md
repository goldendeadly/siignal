**Topic:** The Shift from Chatbots to Autonomous Agents in 2026

**Core Thesis:** In 2026, the AI industry is moving beyond conversational chatbots (like ChatGPT) toward autonomous agents that can plan, use tools, and execute complex workflows with minimal human intervention. This shift represents the transition from "AI as a tool" to "AI as a teammate."

**Key Points:**
1. Chatbots are reactive; agents are proactive.
2. The rise of "Agentic Workflows" — iterative loops of planning, execution, and reflection.
3. Tool use (browsing, code execution, API calls) is the defining feature of true agents.
4. Economic impact: Agents will handle end-to-end business processes (marketing, sales, dev), not just discrete tasks.
5. The "Human-in-the-Loop" model is evolving into "Human-as-the-Architect."
