## Product Summary

SolarEdge Pro is a premium residential rooftop solar installation service targeting homeowners in Sun Belt states. The offer includes a complete end-to-end experience: free energy audit, custom system design, professional installation within 5 business days, and a 25-year performance warranty. The financing model eliminates the upfront cost barrier with $0 down and immediate monthly savings.

The current promotional incentive is a free smart home energy monitor (valued at $499) for installations booked in June 2026.

## Target Audience Persona

**Primary Persona: "Practical Paul"**

Paul is a 45-year-old homeowner in suburban Phoenix, AZ. He earns $110K/year, owns a 2,200 sq ft home, and is tired of paying $280+/month in electricity during summer. He is not an environmentalist by identity but appreciates doing the right thing when it also saves money. He has been approached by solar salespeople before but found them pushy and untrustworthy. He researches purchases thoroughly before committing and values data, warranties, and company reputation over flashy marketing.

**Demographics:** Homeowners, 35–60, HHI $85K+, Sun Belt (TX, AZ, FL, CA, NV).

**Psychographics:** Pragmatic, research-oriented, value-driven, skeptical of hype, responsive to authority and social proof.

## Objections Register

| Objection | Handling Strategy |

| "Will it damage my roof?" | Emphasize professional installation, roof warranty coverage, and ironclad workmanship guarantee. |
| "Solar companies go out of business" | Stress 25-year warranty backed by insurance policy; company financial stability indicators. |
| "Upfront cost is too high" | Lead with $0 down financing; reframe as "lower your bill from day one." |
