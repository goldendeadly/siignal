# Run Brief — Premium Rooftop Solar Installation

## Campaign Details
- **Run Date:** 2026-05-27
- **Campaign Name / Product:** SolarEdge Pro — Premium Rooftop Solar Installation
- **Slug / Identifier:** solaredge-pro-launch

## Offer Information
- **Product or Service Description:** SolarEdge Pro is a premium residential rooftop solar installation service targeting homeowners in the Sun Belt region. The service includes a free energy audit, custom system design, professional installation, 25-year warranty, and a financing plan with $0 down.
- **Unique Selling Points (USPs):**
  - 25-year performance warranty (industry-leading)
  - $0 down financing with savings from month one
  - Average customer saves $2,400/year on electricity
  - Installation completed in under 5 business days
  - Tier-1 panels with 22.5% efficiency rating
- **Price / Promotion:** Average system cost $28,000 before incentives; after federal tax credit and state rebates, effective cost is $14,000–$18,000. Current promotion: Free smart home energy monitor ($499 value) with every installation booked in June.

## Audience & Persona
- **Target Audience:** Homeowners aged 35–60, household income $85K+, residing in Sun Belt states (TX, AZ, FL, CA, NV). Environmentally conscious but primarily motivated by cost savings. Likely owns their home (no renters). Tech-comfortable but not early adopters.
- **Pain Points:**
  - Rising electricity bills (averaging $250+/month)
  - Distrust of solar companies due to aggressive sales tactics in the market
  - Uncertainty about ROI and payback period
  - Worry about roof damage during installation
- **Desired Outcomes:**
  - Lower monthly energy costs immediately
  - Increase home value
  - Energy independence and protection from rate hikes
  - Feel good about environmental impact without sacrificing comfort
- **Objections & Barriers:**
  - "What if I move before the system pays for itself?"
  - "Will it damage my roof?"
  - "I've heard solar companies go out of business — who honors the warranty?"
  - "The upfront cost is too high"

## Strategy & Goals
- **Resonance Mode / Brand Voice:** Authority (data-driven, trustworthy, no-hype)
- **Marketing Objective:** Lead Generation (book free energy audits)
- **Budget:** $15,000/month across all platforms
- **Campaign Duration:** June 1 – August 31, 2026 (3 months)
- **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
  - Cost per lead (target: < $45)
  - Lead-to-audit conversion rate (target: > 35%)
  - Cost per acquisition (target: < $800)
  - Return on ad spend (target: > 4x)

## Platforms & Channels
- **Facebook/Instagram:** Primary channel. Carousel ads, video testimonials, lead form ads.
- **Google Ads:** Search campaigns targeting high-intent keywords ("solar installation near me," "solar panel cost 2026"). Display retargeting.
- **YouTube:** 30-second pre-roll video ads featuring customer testimonials and savings data.
- **NextDoor:** Community-focused ads leveraging neighbor trust signals.

## Constraints & Notes
- **Compliance / Regulatory Considerations:** All savings claims must include "results may vary" disclaimer. Cannot guarantee specific dollar savings without noting it depends on usage and system size. Must comply with FTC guidelines on testimonials.
- **Brand Guidelines:** Professional, clean aesthetic. Navy blue and white color palette. No aggressive countdown timers or fake urgency. Photography should show real installations, not stock photos.
- **Additional Notes:** Competitor landscape is crowded. Differentiate on trust, warranty length, and speed of installation. Avoid price-war messaging.
