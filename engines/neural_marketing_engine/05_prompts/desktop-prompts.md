# Desktop Prompts – Neural Marketing Engine

Desktop prompts are used for high‑level planning, coordination and summarisation tasks.  They guide the AI to think through complex decisions before diving into content generation or editing.

## How to Use

Use a desktop prompt whenever you need to:

* Assess which context or step to perform next.
* Plan the overall structure of a run (e.g. decide on the resonance mode, outcome mapping or keyword focus).
* Summarise feedback and decide on optimisation strategies.

## Template

```
**Situation:** Briefly describe what you’ve done so far and what remains.

**Goal:** State the immediate goal (e.g. generate titles, revise ads).

**Considerations:** List any constraints or important factors (tone, avatar, deadlines, performance metrics).

**Plan:** Outline the steps you will take next, citing the context or template you will use.

**Decision:** If a decision is needed (e.g. choose a blog title), list the options with pros/cons and indicate your choice.
```

This template helps structure your thinking so that subsequent actions are clear and justified.
