# SEO Intent Template

Map out the search intent and keyword strategy for your campaign.  Use this template to ensure that your blog post and landing page align with your desired search audience.

## Search Intent

Select one or describe a hybrid:

* **Informational** – users want to learn about a topic.
* **Transactional** – users are ready to purchase or take action.
* **Navigational** – users are looking for a specific brand or website.
* **Comparison** – users want to compare options.

## Primary Keyword

Enter the main keyword you wish to rank for.

## LSI / Secondary Keywords

List 3–5 related keywords or phrases that support your primary keyword.  These may include semantic variations or longer phrases.

## Long‑Tail Queries

Identify 2–3 longer phrases or questions your avatar might type into a search engine.  These queries should map to subsections of your blog post.

## Competitor References

List any competitor articles or landing pages you have analysed.  Note their strengths and weaknesses and how you plan to differentiate.
