## Psychographics

* **Values and Beliefs:** What principles or causes does the avatar care about?
* **Lifestyle & Interests:** Hobbies, media consumption habits, communities.
* **Personality Traits:** Introverted/extraverted, logical/emotional, adventurous/cautious.

## Pain Points

List the frustrations, fears or challenges the avatar experiences related to your niche.  Use their language and be specific.

## Desires and Aspirations

What does the avatar want to achieve?  What transformation are they seeking?  Include both tangible outcomes (e.g. “save money”) and intangible outcomes (e.g. “feel secure”).

## Objections and Barriers

Identify common reasons the avatar might hesitate to buy (price, time commitment, scepticism).  Include ways to address these objections in your messaging.

## Preferred Content Formats

How does the avatar prefer to consume information?  Videos, podcasts, articles, infographics, emails, social media?  Note any platforms they frequent.

## Buying Triggers

Events or circumstances that prompt the avatar to seek a solution (e.g. seasonal changes, life milestones, market shifts).
