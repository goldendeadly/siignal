# Signal Expansion Context

## Purpose

Transform the insights from the strategy model and blog post into a **multi‑platform social signal**.  This context generates content tailored to the unique formats and norms of various platforms, ensuring consistent messaging while maximising reach and engagement.

## Read First

* The strategy model (`04_workspaces/strategy-modeling/`).
* The long‑form blog post (`04_workspaces/blog-generation/`).
* The social signal template (`06_templates/social-signal-template.md`).

## Use This Context For

* Identifying modular insights from the blog that can stand alone as social posts.
* Crafting platform‑specific content:
  * **Twitter/X** – threads or tweet storms with hooks, insights and CTAs.
  * **LinkedIn** – articles or carousel posts emphasising professional value.
  * **Instagram** – carousels or reel scripts with visuals and captions.
  * **YouTube / TikTok** – short video scripts outlining key points.
  * **Other** – any additional platforms relevant to the niche.
* Including appropriate hashtags, mentions, emojis and tone adjustments for each platform.

## Ignore

* Do not write ads or landing pages here.
* Do not revise the blog post except to break it into snippets.

## Expected Outputs

* A markdown file called `[run-slug]-social-signal-expansion-v1.md` containing the social signal set for all target platforms.  Save it in `04_workspaces/signal-expansion/`.
