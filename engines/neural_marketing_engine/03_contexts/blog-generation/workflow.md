# Blog Generation Workflow

1. **Review the strategy model**
   * Read the avatar insights, offer positioning, tone guidelines, outcome mapping and SEO cluster.
   * Make note of the primary keyword and any LSI terms to weave into titles and the post.

2. **Generate blog titles**
   * Brainstorm at least seven possible headlines.  Use the pain points and desired transformation to craft hooks.  Vary formats (how‑to, listicle, question, case study, contrarian).
   * Ensure each title is no longer than 70 characters and includes the primary keyword when possible.
   * Save the list in a markdown file named `[run-slug]-blog-titles-generation-v1.md`.

3. **Select or rank titles**
   * If the operator needs assistance choosing a title, rank them based on clarity, appeal and keyword inclusion.
   * Note the selected title in the titles file or a separate selection note.

4. **Write the long‑form blog post**
   * Use the selected title as the post’s headline.
   * Outline the post: introduction (problem statement and hook), body (main sections), conclusion (summary and CTA).
   * Write 1,500–2,500 words following the tone and outcome guidance.  Use storytelling or technical explanation as appropriate.
   * Integrate the primary keyword and LSI terms naturally.  Include at least one CTA encouraging readers to explore the offer.
   * Save the post as `[run-slug]-blog-post-generation-v1.md` in `04_workspaces/blog-generation/`.

5. **Mark completion**
   * Once the titles and post are written and saved, proceed to the `signal-expansion` context.
