## Week 1 Tuning Allowance (Scope Control)

During the first 7 days of deployment:
- Up to 3 minor adjustments to greeting, routing, or qualification rules are included at no charge.
- Major scope changes (new meeting types, new service areas, new integrations) are quoted separately.
- Tuning requests must be submitted via email or shared doc (not verbal).
