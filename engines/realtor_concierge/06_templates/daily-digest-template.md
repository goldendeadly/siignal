# Daily Digest Template – Realtor Concierge Engine

Use this template to compile the daily summary email.  Populate the bracketed placeholders with actual values for the date, counts and lists.  The digest provides the team with a concise overview of the day’s activity and required actions.

```
Subject: Daily Concierge Summary — [DATE] — [TEAM NAME]

Reporting Window: [DATE], [START TIME]–[END TIME] ([TIME ZONE])
Service Area: [CITY/REGION]
Follow‑up Owner: [AGENT NAME / TEAM INBOX]
Hot Lead Alert Channel: [SMS and/or Email]
=====================================================================
TOPLINE SUMMARY
=====================================================================
New inbound leads today: [Total calls]
Booked appointments: [Number booked]
Hot leads needing personal follow‑up: [Number hot]
Warm/Nurture needing follow‑up: [Number warm/nurture]
Disqualified: [Number disqualified]
Lead leaks (missed calls not reached): [Number lead leaks]
Incomplete (Partial/Insufficient): [Number incomplete]
=====================================================================
2) BOOKED APPOINTMENTS (Action: Confirm / Prepare)
[For each booked appointment within the next 48 hours, list:]
- Lead ID: [Log_ID]
  Name: [Name]
  Phone: [Phone]
  Email: [Email]
  Intent: [Buyer/Seller]
  Temperature: [HOT/WARM]
  Appointment: [Buyer/Seller Consult] — [Date Time]
  Next Step Date/Time: [Date Time]
  Outcome: [Outcome]
  Notes:
  [SAID] [Key statements]
  Owner Action: [e.g. Arrive with 2–3 listings; confirm property details]

3) HOT LEADS (Immediate Follow‑Up Required)
[For each hot lead that is not yet booked, list:]
- Lead ID: [Log_ID]
  Name: [Name]
  Phone: [Phone]
  Email: [Email]
  Intent: [Buyer/Seller]
  Temperature: HOT
  Next Step Date/Time: [Date Time]
  Outcome: [Outcome]
  Notes:
  [SAID] [Key statements]
  Owner Action: [Call within 15 minutes; offer consult or confirm booking]

4) CALLBACKS NEEDED (Not Booked)
[For each lead with outcome = Callback Requested, list:]
- Name: [Name]
  Phone: [Phone]
  Reason: [Reason for callback]
  Recommended Next Action + Due Time: [e.g. Call back by 3 PM; confirm appointment options]

5) AT‑RISK / INCOMPLETE (Fix Data Gaps)
[For each call with Completeness=PARTIAL or INSUFFICIENT, list:]
- Name/Phone: [Name] – [Phone]
  Missing Info: [e.g. area, timeline]
  Action to Resolve: [e.g. Call to capture missing info]

6) DISQUALIFIED (Count + Top Reasons)
Total disqualified: [Number]
Top reasons:
- [Reason 1] – [Count]
- [Reason 2] – [Count]
- [Reason 3] – [Count]

7) NOTES SNAPSHOT (Top 10 Only)
[List up to 10 notes summary entries:]
- [Name] | [Temperature] | [Notes summary] | [Next action]
```

Fill in all placeholders with the actual numbers and lists before sending.
