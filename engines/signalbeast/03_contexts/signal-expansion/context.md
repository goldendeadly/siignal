# Signal Expansion Context

## Purpose

The **Signal Expansion** context is dedicated to growing the impact of the content beyond the initial set of outputs.  In this phase you identify new topics, collaboration opportunities, backlink targets, lead magnet ideas and system improvements that build upon the current run.  This corresponds to Phase 5 of SignalBeast.

## Read First

- The analytics and memory feedback documents produced in the Tracking & Canon phase.
- All platform outputs and highlights from previous phases.
- Any expansion guidelines or inspiration documents stored in `01_source-material/`.

## Use This Context For

- Generating new blog topic ideas based on high‑performing keywords and user engagement.
- Finding potential collaborators (influencers, brands, communities) who align with the subject matter.
- Identifying high‑quality sites or platforms where backlinks could be acquired.
- Brainstorming lead magnet concepts (e.g. checklists, templates, infographics, calculators) relevant to the topic.
- Suggesting improvements or automations to enhance the next run.

## Ignore

- Writing new platform content (handled in the Platform Explosion or Recursion Engine context).
- Detailed automation workflows (covered in the Automation Suggestions phase).

## Expected Outputs

- A list of 5 new blog or content topics derived from successful keywords and engagement signals.
- A list of 3 potential collaborators or backlink targets with a short rationale for each.
- One lead magnet idea with a brief description of its format and value.
- One suggested system improvement or automation idea to streamline future runs.
