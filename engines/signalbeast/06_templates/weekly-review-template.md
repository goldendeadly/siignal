# Weekly Review Template

Use this template to conduct a weekly reflection on SignalBeast activities.  It helps ensure continuous improvement and alignment with strategic goals.

## Weekly Summary

- **Week of:** (YYYY‑MM‑DD to YYYY‑MM‑DD)
- **Runs Completed:** List the run IDs or topics worked on during the week.
- **Highlights:** Summarise major achievements (e.g. number of outputs generated, top‑performing content, successful collaborations).
- **Challenges:** Note any obstacles or bottlenecks encountered.

## Metrics Overview

Provide a snapshot of the key metrics for the week (CTR, engagement rate, reach, leads generated, new backlinks, etc.).  Use bullet points or short phrases.

## Lessons Learned

Reflect on what worked well, what didn’t and why.  Identify patterns or insights that could inform future runs.

## Action Items

List the actions to carry forward into the next week (e.g. adjust resonance mode, refine hashtags, contact collaborators, implement automation).  Assign owners and due dates if applicable.
