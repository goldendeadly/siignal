# Platform Outputs Tracker Template

Use this template to track every individual piece of content generated across platforms.  Each row should represent a single output (e.g. one tweet, one LinkedIn article, one carousel).  Capture short, relevant details; do not write lengthy descriptions in the table.

## Fields

- **Run ID:** Link back to the Content Master Tracker.
- **Platform:** e.g. Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, Quora, Facebook, Pinterest, Medium, Substack, Blogger, Dev.to, Hashnode.
- **Output Type:** Thread, Post, Carousel, Reel, Video Script, Answer, Article, etc.
- **Headline / Hook:** The first line or key phrase of the piece.
- **Keywords / Hashtags:** Primary and secondary keywords or hashtag set.
- **CTA:** The call‑to‑action used (Soft, Engagement, Conversion) and text of the CTA.
- **Canonical Link:** The canonical blog URL included in the output.
- **Publish Date:** Planned or actual date/time of publication.
- **Status:** Draft, Review, Scheduled, Published, Archived.
- **Engagement %:** Optional performance metric (to be filled post‑publication).
- **CTR %:** Click‑through rate (if applicable).
- **Resonance Score (0–10):** Manual or calculated measure of how well the tone landed.
