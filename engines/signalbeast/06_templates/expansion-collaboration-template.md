# Expansion & Collaboration Template

This template provides a structure for documenting new topics, collaboration opportunities, backlink targets, lead magnet ideas and system improvements derived from the Signal Expansion phase.

## Sections

### New Blog Ideas

List five potential blog or content topics inspired by high‑performing keywords and audience questions.  Keep each idea to a short phrase or sentence.

### Collaborators & Backlink Targets

Identify three people, brands or publications that would make good partners or backlink sources.  For each, include a brief rationale (e.g. audience alignment, expertise, potential mutual benefit).

### Lead Magnet Idea

Describe one resource (e.g. checklist, infographic, template, calculator) that could be offered to the audience.  Outline its core promise and how it connects to the main topic.

### System Improvement

Suggest one process change or automation that would improve future runs.  Provide a concise description of the problem and how the improvement would solve it.
