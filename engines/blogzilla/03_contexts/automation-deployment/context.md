# Automation & Deployment Context

## Purpose

Prepare the generated content for publication and automate the distribution workflow. This context handles scheduling, VA/Canva instructions, integration with scheduling tools and final QA before posts go live.

## Read Before Starting

- The approved platform outputs from `platform-generation`.
- The run’s publishing cadence and platform priorities from the run brief and analysis.
- Any existing automation SOPs or templates in `06_templates/`.

## Ignore

- Raw drafts or unapproved content.
- Analytics until after publishing.

## Expected Outputs

- Publishing schedule detailing platform, post dates/times, content files and CTA types.
- VA/Canva guidance documents summarising design requirements for carousels, reels or videos.
- Automation notes specifying which posts should be queued in scheduling tools (e.g. Buffer, Hypefury) and which tasks require manual intervention.
