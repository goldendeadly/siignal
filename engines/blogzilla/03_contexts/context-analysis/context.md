# Context Analysis

## Purpose

Analyse the source material to understand its tone, audience intent, conversion goal and SEO structure. This context produces the canonical statement and the semantic SEO map (primary keyword, LSI keywords, long‑tail variants and related questions), along with suggestions for publication cadence and content silo.

## Read Before Starting

- The run brief generated in source intake.
- The source blog post or keyword/idea.
- Any relevant research or examples in `01_source-material/`.

## Ignore

- Completed outputs or drafts from other contexts.
- Old analytics or tracker files unless needed for reference.

## Expected Outputs

- Tone analysis (e.g. educational, narrative, technical, philosophical, persuasive).
- Audience intent and emotional frequency profile.
- Conversion goal classification (awareness, authority, leads, retention).
- Primary keyword and 3–5 LSI/secondary keywords.
- 2–3 suggested long‑tail variants.
- Related search questions and latent topic gaps.
- Suggested publication frequency and content silo category.
- Canonical statement referencing the original blog.
