# Amplification & Expansion Workflow

1. **Review outputs** – read the generated platform drafts and identify themes or insights that resonated strongly.
2. **Analyse performance indicators** – if available, check early metrics or feedback (e.g. high‑engagement highlights) to see which topics gained traction.
3. **Generate new topic ideas** – propose five new blog topics that build on the successful elements of the current run. Aim for a mix of long‑tail keyword focus and broader thematic exploration.
4. **Identify collaborators** – list three potential collaborators or backlink opportunities: content creators, influencers, websites or communities relevant to the topic.
5. **Suggest lead magnet** – devise one downloadable resource (checklist, ebook, template, toolkit) that complements the current content and encourages lead capture.
6. **Recommend automation/system improvement** – suggest one improvement to streamline future runs (e.g. template enhancements, automation scripts, process changes).
7. **Save outputs** – record the new topics, collaborators, backlink targets, lead magnet and improvement notes in separate files (`new-topics.md`, `collaboration-opps.md`, `backlink-opps.md`, `lead-magnet.md`, `automation-improvement.md`) within the run folder.
8. **Hand off** – route to `tracking-memory` for logging or to `automation-deployment` if immediate scheduling is needed.
