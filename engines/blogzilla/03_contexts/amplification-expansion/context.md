# Amplification & Expansion Context

## Purpose

Identify opportunities to expand the impact of the source content beyond the initial outputs. This context generates new blog ideas, collaboration and backlink opportunities, lead magnet suggestions and automation improvements. It focuses on growth and future cycles rather than immediate output.

## Read Before Starting

- The outputs generated in `platform-generation` for context.
- The analysis and core generation files for reference.

## Ignore

- Routine tracking tasks (handled in `tracking-memory`).
- Deployment instructions (handled in `automation-deployment`).

## Expected Outputs

- Five new blog topic ideas based on high‑performing keywords and insights.
- Three collaboration or backlink opportunities (e.g. influencers, guest posts, resource swaps).
- One lead magnet idea (e.g. downloadable resource, checklist, toolkit).
- One automation or system improvement suggestion for the next cycle.
