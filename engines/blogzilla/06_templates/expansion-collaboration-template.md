# Expansion & Collaboration Template

Use this template to record ideas and opportunities generated during the amplification & expansion phase.

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| New Blog Ideas | List of five topics with brief descriptions and why they are relevant |
| Potential Collaborators | Names or handles of creators, sites or communities to partner with |
| Backlink Targets | Sites or posts where backlinks could be placed |
| Lead Magnet Idea | Short description of a downloadable resource or offer |
| System Improvement Notes | Suggestions for process improvements or automation |

Document each suggestion clearly and include links or research notes where applicable. These records inform future runs and outreach efforts.
