# Desktop Prompts

These prompts are intended for planning and high‑level coordination tasks in a desktop or chat environment.  Use them to frame the campaign and manage progress.

## Campaign Planning Prompt

> **Objective:** Plan the overall advertising campaign run.
>
> **Read:**
> - The run brief in `07_inputs/run-briefs/[run-file].md`.
> - `offer-details.md` and `market-analysis-v1.md` for context.
>
> **Tasks:**
> - Confirm the resonance mode, marketing objective and selected platforms.
> - Outline the seven AdZilla phases (offer intake, market analysis, creative generation, ad assembly, targeting plan, performance tracking, optimisation & expansion).
> - Identify dependencies and the order of work across contexts.
> - Set initial deadlines and assign ownership if working with a team.
>
> **Output:** A concise campaign plan summarising objectives, platform selection, context order and key milestones.

## Progress Coordination Prompt

> **Objective:** Coordinate progress across multiple contexts and collaborators.
>
> **Read:**
> - The campaign plan.
> - Current state of run folders in `04_workspaces/`.
>
> **Tasks:**
> - Identify which phases are complete, in progress or pending.
> - Note any blockers (e.g. missing analysis, incomplete ad drafts) and assign resolution steps.
> - Update the campaign plan and provide a status summary to stakeholders.
>
> **Output:** An updated progress report outlining completed tasks, outstanding items and next steps.
