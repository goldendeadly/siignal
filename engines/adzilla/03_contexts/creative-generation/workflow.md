# Creative Generation Workflow

This workflow outlines how to transform your market analysis into compelling messaging assets.

## Steps

1. **Review market analysis**
   - Read `market-analysis-v1.md` to refresh your understanding of the audience, competitors, regulations, brand voice and key benefits.
   - Keep the resonance mode and marketing objective in mind.

2. **Write value propositions**
   - Summarise the main reason the audience should be interested in the offer in one or two sentences.
   - Ensure each value proposition is clear, benefit‑oriented and differentiates the offer from competitors.
   - Craft at least two but no more than three distinct value propositions to test.

3. **List benefit bullets**
   - Generate 5–10 bullet points highlighting specific features and advantages.
   - Frame each bullet as a benefit to the audience (e.g. “Save time with…”, “Gain peace of mind thanks to…”).
   - Keep bullets concise; avoid jargon.

4. **Generate hooks**
   - Write several attention‑grabbing opening lines or questions (hooks) that can introduce an ad.
   - Use techniques such as curiosity gaps, surprising statistics, rhetorical questions or bold statements.
   - Ensure the hook aligns with the resonance mode (e.g. authoritative facts, emotional appeals, technical insights).

5. **Create taglines**
   - Craft short slogans or taglines summarising the offer’s promise in a memorable way.
   - Keep them under 10 words if possible.
   - Consider alliteration, rhyme or parallel structure for memorability.

6. **Save assets**
   - Write `value-propositions.md` with each value proposition on its own line or section.
   - Write `benefits.md` with bullet points for each benefit.
   - Write `hooks-taglines.md` with separate sections: one for hooks and one for taglines.
   - Save these files in `04_workspaces/creative-generation/[run-folder]/`.

7. **Hand off to Ad Assembly**
   - Once core assets are complete, proceed to the Ad Assembly context to turn them into platform‑specific ad copy.

## Outputs

- Three messaging files: `value-propositions.md`, `benefits.md` and `hooks-taglines.md`.
