# Optimisation & Expansion Context

## Purpose

The **Optimisation & Expansion** context analyses performance data to propose improvements and additional campaign opportunities.  It focuses on testing, retargeting, cross‑selling and automation suggestions to make future campaigns more effective and efficient.

## Read First

- The performance reflections document (`performance-reflections-v1.md`).
- Updated tracker files with metrics and performance scores.
- Market analysis and messaging assets to recall key insights.

## Use This Context For

- Identifying top‑performing ads and elements (hooks, value propositions, audiences) and analysing why they worked.
- Proposing **A/B tests** for headlines, body copy, CTAs, creatives and audience segments to validate hypotheses.
- Designing **retargeting campaigns** (e.g. targeting website visitors, engaged users, abandoned cart users, lookalike audiences).
- Suggesting **cross‑selling or up‑selling** opportunities based on product relationships.
- Recommending **automation** or tool enhancements (e.g. automated bidding, rule‑based budget adjustments, scheduling automation, creative rotation scripts).

## Ignore

- Writing new ad copy (unless part of an A/B test suggestion).
- Detailed tracking (handled in the Performance Tracking context).

## Expected Outputs

- `optimisation-expansion-v1.md` – A report containing proposed tests, retargeting strategies, cross‑sell ideas and automation recommendations.
- Updated run plan or campaign schedule if changes are implemented.
