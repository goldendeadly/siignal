# Optimisation & Expansion Workflow

This workflow outlines how to propose and document improvements and extensions to the campaign after reviewing performance data.

## Steps

1. **Review reflections and metrics**
   - Read `performance-reflections-v1.md` and the tracker files to understand what performed well and what didn’t.
   - Note which hooks, value propositions, audiences or platforms drove the best results.

2. **Identify areas for testing**
   - Choose messaging elements (headlines, CTAs, body copy) or creative concepts that could be tested.
   - Define the hypothesis for each test (e.g. “Shorter headlines will increase CTR”; “A question hook will outperform a statement hook”).
   - Decide how many variants to test and what metrics will determine success.

3. **Design retargeting campaigns**
   - Identify warm audiences (website visitors, users who watched videos, engaged social media users) and craft offers or messages tailored to them.
   - Suggest lookalike audiences based on top converters.
   - Outline retargeting campaign structure (ad types, budgets, durations) for each platform.

4. **Propose cross‑sell and up‑sell opportunities**
   - Look at the product ecosystem to find complementary or higher‑tier offerings.
   - Suggest how to position these offerings to people who converted or showed interest in the initial offer.

5. **Recommend automation improvements**
   - Based on manual tasks identified in the run, suggest automation tools or scripts to streamline reporting, budgeting, bidding or creative rotation.
   - Provide a short description of each tool’s function and how it will be integrated (e.g. using a platform API, Zapier, or a built‑in feature).

6. **Document the optimisation plan**
   - Write `optimisation-expansion-v1.md` with sections for A/B tests, retargeting strategies, cross‑selling ideas and automation recommendations.
   - For each item, include the reasoning, expected outcome and any prerequisites.

7. **Implement or schedule tests**
   - If time permits and resources are available, schedule the proposed tests and retargeting campaigns.
   - Otherwise, document them for future runs.

## Outputs

- A detailed optimisation and expansion report to guide future campaign iterations.
