# Ad Assembly Workflow

This workflow guides the creation of platform‑specific ad copy based on the messaging assets.

## Steps

1. **Review messaging assets**
   - Open `value-propositions.md`, `benefits.md` and `hooks-taglines.md`.
   - Choose the messaging elements most appropriate for the selected resonance mode and audience.

2. **Select platforms**
   - Confirm which platforms to target based on the run brief and market analysis (e.g. Facebook, Instagram, Google Ads, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok).
   - Create a plan for the number and type of ads per platform (e.g. 3 Facebook ads, 2 Instagram ads, 1 LinkedIn sponsored post).

3. **Write platform‑specific ads**
   - **Facebook & Instagram:** Write a headline (25–30 characters), a short body (90–110 characters for primary text) and a CTA (“Learn More”, “Get Offer”, etc.).  Suggest imagery or video themes (e.g. lifestyle, product close‑up, benefit demonstration).
   - **Google Ads:** Compose headlines (3 × 30 characters), descriptions (2 × 90 characters) and path fields.  Use dynamic keyword insertion if applicable.
   - **LinkedIn:** Craft a sponsored post (75–150 words) or a sponsored content headline and summary.  Keep tone professional yet engaging.
   - **YouTube & TikTok:** Outline a short pre‑roll script or caption (≤15 seconds) highlighting the hook and value proposition.  Suggest visual storytelling elements.
   - **Other platforms:** Adapt as needed (e.g. Twitter Ads: 280 characters including hashtags; Reddit Ads: concise headline and text; etc.).
   - Always include a CTA and the canonical brand line.

4. **Save ad files**
   - For each platform and ad variation, create a file named `[product]-[platform]-ad-draft-v1.md` in `04_workspaces/ad-assembly/[run-folder]/`.
   - Within each file, clearly label sections: Headline, Body, CTA, Creative Notes, Canonical Line.

5. **Review and iterate**
   - Read each ad aloud and test for clarity, tone and compliance with platform policies.
   - Make any necessary adjustments to ensure messaging is consistent and compelling.
   - Version up to `v2` or `final` as edits are made.

6. **Hand off to Targeting Plan**
   - Once ads are written and approved, proceed to the Targeting Plan context to define audience segments and budgets.

## Outputs

- Platform‑specific ad files ready for review and targeting.
