# Targeting Plan Context

## Purpose

The **Targeting Plan** context defines how and to whom your ads will be delivered.  It covers audience segmentation, geographic targeting, budget allocations, scheduling and optimization goals for each platform.

## Read First

- The run brief for budget, duration and platform selection.
- Audience persona insights from `market-analysis-v1.md`.
- Any existing targeting guidelines or past campaign data in `01_source-material/`.

## Use This Context For

- Selecting audience segments based on demographics, interests, behaviours and lookalike characteristics.
- Defining geographic targeting (country, region, city, radius around locations).
- Allocating budgets across platforms and campaigns (daily vs. lifetime budgets, ad set budgets).
- Setting schedules (start/end dates, dayparting for specific time windows).
- Choosing optimization goals (e.g. clicks, conversions, leads, video views) and bidding strategies.

## Ignore

- Writing ad copy (handled in Ad Assembly).
- Performance tracking (handled later).

## Expected Outputs

- `targeting-plan-v1.md` – A document outlining audience segments, geographic parameters, budget allocation, schedules and optimization goals for each platform.
