# Market Analysis Context

## Purpose

The **Market Analysis** context explores the environment in which your offer will compete.  It investigates the target audience’s demographics and psychographics, examines competitor messaging and positioning, and identifies regulatory considerations.  This research informs the creation of value propositions and hooks in the next phase.

## Read First

- `offer-details.md` from the offer-intake workspace.
- Run brief for reference on resonance mode and objectives.
- Any competitor ads, industry reports or market research documents provided in `01_source-material/`.

## Use This Context For

- Analysing the audience persona in depth: demographics (age, gender, location, income), psychographics (values, beliefs, lifestyle), pain points and desired transformations.
- Researching competitor positioning, messaging and offers to identify gaps and opportunities.
- Identifying regulatory constraints or platform policies relevant to your industry and offer.
- Clarifying the brand voice and resonance patterns (authority, emotional, technical, viral, educational, hybrid).
- Summarising key benefits and differentiators that will later become hooks and value statements.

## Ignore

- Writing copy or crafting hooks (this belongs to Creative Generation).
- Developing targeting plans or budgets (handled in later contexts).

## Expected Outputs

- `market-analysis-v1.md` – A document that compiles audience insights, competitor highlights, regulatory notes, brand voice guidelines and a list of key benefits/differentiators.
