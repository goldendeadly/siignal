# Market Analysis Workflow

This workflow guides the systematic analysis of the audience and competitive landscape during the Market Analysis phase.

## Steps

1. **Review offer details**
   - Open `offer-details.md` and recap the product features, benefits, target audience and constraints.
   - Note the resonance mode and marketing objective.

2. **Deep dive into the audience persona**
   - For demographics, capture age ranges, gender distribution, location, education level, occupation, income and family status.
   - For psychographics, describe values, attitudes, interests, lifestyle, motivations and fears.
   - Identify the key pain points the audience experiences and the benefits they are seeking.
   - Note any objections they may have to the offer.

3. **Competitor analysis**
   - Identify major competitors and examine their messaging, positioning and offers.
   - Note their pricing strategy, USPs, tone of voice and advertising channels.
   - Highlight areas where your offer differs or can stand out.

4. **Regulatory and compliance considerations**
   - List any industry regulations (e.g. health claims, financial disclosures, advertising standards) that apply to the offer.
   - Summarise platform advertising policies relevant to the campaign.

5. **Define brand voice and resonance**
   - Clarify how the brand should speak (confident and authoritative, warm and emotional, logical and technical, etc.).
   - Align the resonance mode with the target audience preferences and campaign objectives.

6. **List benefits and differentiators**
   - Based on the offer details and competitive analysis, list the top benefits and unique selling points that differentiate the offer.
   - Rank them according to audience importance and potential impact.

7. **Create the market analysis document**
   - Write `market-analysis-v1.md` summarising all findings: audience insights, competitor highlights, regulatory notes, brand voice guidelines and key benefits/differentiators.
   - Use clear sections and bullet points for readability.

## Outputs

- `market-analysis-v1.md` capturing all relevant research and analysis for the creative generation phase.
