# Offer Intake Context

## Purpose

The **Offer Intake** context initiates a new AdZilla run.  Its primary job is to collect and organise all necessary information about the offer, target audience, objectives, budgets and platform selection.  A well‑structured intake ensures that subsequent phases have the context they need to generate effective advertising copy.

## Read First

- The run brief located in `07_inputs/run-briefs/`.
- Offer descriptions, product brochures or service outlines provided in `07_inputs/`.

## Use This Context For

- Verifying and documenting the resonance mode and marketing objective.
- Recording the product or service description, unique selling points, pricing, features and benefits.
- Capturing the target audience persona, including demographics, psychographics, pain points and desires.
- Noting any constraints such as budget, timeline, regulatory considerations or brand guidelines.
- Creating a run folder and storing the intake documentation.

## Ignore

- Analysis of competitors and market (handled in the Market Analysis context).
- Drafting creative assets or ads (handled in later contexts).

## Expected Outputs

- `offer-details.md` – A structured file containing the offer information, marketing objective, resonance mode, target audience and constraints.
- A run folder created in `04_workspaces/offer-intake/run-YYYY-MM-DD-product/`.
